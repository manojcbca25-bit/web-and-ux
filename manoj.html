Curated Destinations

Top 12 hand-picked locations for the ultimate Bengaluru experience.

Nature

Cubbon Park

The "Lungs of the City." A massive 300-acre green space perfect for morning jogs and visiting the magnificent State Library.

Central Bengaluru ★ 4.8
History

Bangalore Palace

Inspired by Windsor Castle, this architectural marvel features Tudor-style architecture and elegant wood carvings.

Vasanth Nagar ★ 4.6
Nature

Lalbagh Gardens

Famous for its iconic 19th-century glass house and bi-annual flower shows. Home to India's largest collection of tropical plants.

Mavalli ★ 4.7
Shopping

Commercial Street

The ultimate shopping destination. From branded clothes to dirt-cheap trinkets, find everything here if you know how to bargain.

Shivaji Nagar ★ 4.5
Spiritual

ISKCON Temple

A spiritual landmark on Hare Krishna Hill. One of the largest ISKCON temples in the world with stunning modern architecture.

Rajajinagar ★ 4.9
Gastronomy

V.V. Puram Food Street

A vegetarian's paradise. At sunset, this street comes alive with stalls selling Gobi Manchurian, Dosa, and Floating Pani Puri.

Basavanagudi ★ 4.8
Wildlife

Bannerghatta Safari

Experience the wild side. Famous for its tiger and lion safaris, butterfly park, and a zoo set in the midst of a forest.

Bannerghatta Road ★ 4.6
Luxury

UB City

India's first luxury mall. Visit for high-end fashion brands, fine dining rooftop restaurants, and stunning modern architecture.

Vittal Mallya Road ★ 4.7
Heritage

Tipu Sultan's Palace

An exquisite example of Indo-Islamic architecture. Built entirely of teak wood, this was the summer retreat of the ruler of Mysore.

Chamrajpet ★ 4.4
Nightlife

M.G. Road & Brigade

The beating heart of Bangalore's nightlife. Pubs, cafes, bookstores, and neon lights make this the place to be after dark.

Central Business Dist ★ 4.8
Culture

Malleswaram

Step back into Old Bangalore. Known for its ancient temples, flower markets, and the best filter coffee in the city.

North Bengaluru ★ 4.9
Leisure

Ulsoor Lake

One of the biggest lakes in the city. Rent a boat, take a walk on the islands, or visit the nearby Gurudwara.

Ulsoor ★ 4.3
